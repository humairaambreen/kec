About Krishna Engineering College

Krishna Engineering College (KEC) is a premier institute committed to excellence in engineering education. Located in Bhilai, the college provides an environment that fosters innovation, research, and holistic learning. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, a dedicated faculty, and a student-centered approach, KEC shapes the future of young minds.

About the Project

This website is a demonstration project designed and developed by Humaira Ambreen, a dedicated student of Krishna Engineering College. The project highlights modern web development practices, combining intuitive UI/UX design with cutting-edge technologies to create a seamless user experience.

Features of the Project

  • ID Card Generator: A tool to create personalized student ID cards with details like name, gender, date of birth, batch, and branch.
  • Online Library: Access a collection of e-books, research papers, and academic resources available to students and faculty.
  • Faculty Directory: A comprehensive directory of faculty members, including their contact details, department information, and office hours.
